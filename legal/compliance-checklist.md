# ObjectaLabs Legal & Compliance Checklist

## Overview

This document outlines the legal and compliance requirements for ObjectaLabs. Compliance is critical for building trust and serving enterprise customers.

---

## Company Formation

### Business Structure
- [ ] Choose entity type (Delaware C-Corp recommended)
- [ ] File Certificate of Incorporation

- [ ] File 83(b) elections (within 30 days of stock grant)

### Corporate Governance
- [ ] Board of Directors formation
- [ ] Board meeting schedule
- [ ] Stock option plan (409A valuation)
- [ ] Vesting schedules for founders/employees
- [ ] Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement
- [ ] Confidentiality and Invention Assignment Agreements

### Registrations
- [ ] State business licenses
- [ ] Foreign qualification (if operating in multiple states)
- [ ] Sales tax registration (varies by state)
- [ ] Professional licenses (if required)

## Data Privacy & Security

### GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
**Applies to**: EU residents' data

**Requirements**:
- [ ] Appoint Data Protection Officer (if processing at scale)
- [ ] Conduct Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
- [ ] Implement privacy by design
- [ ] Data processing agreements with vendors
- [ ] Cookie consent mechanism
- [ ] Right to access (data export)
- [ ] Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- [ ] Right to portability
- [ ] Data breach notification (72 hours)
- [ ] Privacy Policy (GDPR-compliant)
- [ ] EU representative (if no EU establishment)

**Documents**:
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Policy
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- Vendor contracts with DPA addendums

### CCPA/CPRA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
**Applies to**: California residents

**Requirements**:
- [ ] Privacy Policy disclosures
- [ ] "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link
- [ ] Right to know (data access)
- [ ] Right to delete
- [ ] Right to opt-out of sale
- [ ] Non-discrimination for exercising rights
- [ ] Authorized agent process

### Other Privacy Laws
- [ ] Virginia CDPA compliance
- [ ] Colorado CPA compliance
- [ ] Other state privacy laws (ongoing monitoring)

### International
- [ ] PIPEDA (Canada)
- [ ] LGPD (Brazil)
- [ ] PDPA (Singapore)
- [ ] Data localization requirements (by country)

## Security Certifications

### SOC 2 Type II
**Timeline**: 12-18 months

**Requirements**:
- [ ] Choose auditor (Big 4 or reputable firm)
- [ ] Gap analysis
- [ ] Implement controls
  - Access controls
  - Change management
  - System monitoring
  - Incident response
  - Vendor management
  - Business continuity
- [ ] 3-6 month observation period
- [ ] Audit execution
- [ ] Report issuance
- [ ] Annual recertification

**Cost**: $50K-$150K

### ISO 27001
**Optional**: More common in Europe

**Requirements**:
- Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- Risk assessment
- Statement of Applicability
- Audit and certification

**Cost**: $30K-$100K

### HIPAA Compliance
**Required for**: Healthcare customers

**Requirements**:
- [ ] Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
- [ ] Risk analysis
- [ ] HIPAA Security Rule compliance
  - Administrative safeguards
  - Physical safeguards
  - Technical safeguards
- [ ] HIPAA Privacy Rule compliance
- [ ] Breach notification procedures
- [ ] Training programs

### PCI DSS
**Required if**: Processing credit cards directly

**Note**: Using Stripe handles this for us

## Insurance

### Recommended Policies

#### General Liability
- Coverage: $1M-$2M
- Protects: Property damage, bodily injury

#### Professional Liability (E&O)
- Coverage: $1M-$5M
- Protects: Errors and omissions, negligence

#### Cyber Liability
- Coverage: $1M-$5M
- Protects: Data breaches, cyber attacks
- Includes: Notification costs, legal fees, PR

#### Directors & Officers (D&O)
- Coverage: $1M-$10M
- Protects: Board and executive decisions
- Required by: Investors

#### Workers' Compensation
- Required by: Law (if employees)
- Coverage: Employee injuries

## Risk Management

### Risk Register

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Data breach | Medium | High | Security measures, insurance, incident response plan |
| GDPR violation | Low | High | Compliance program, DPO, legal review |
