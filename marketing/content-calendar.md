## Blog Post Templates

### Tutorial Post Structure
1. Introduction (problem statement)
2. Prerequisites
3. Step-by-step instructions (with screenshots)
4. Common issues and solutions
5. Next steps
6. CTA

**Length**: 1,500-2,500 words
**Images**: 5-10 screenshots

### Comparison Post Structure
1. Introduction
2. Overview of each option
3. Feature-by-feature comparison (table)
4. Pros and cons
5. Use case recommendations
6. Conclusion and recommendation

**Length**: 2,000-3,000 words
**Visuals**: Comparison tables, charts

### Case Study Structure
1. Customer background
2. Challenge/problem
3. Solution (how they used ObjectaLabs)
4. Implementation process
5. Results (metrics)
6. Key takeaways
7. CTA

**Length**: 1,200-1,800 words
**Visuals**: Results charts, quotes

### Thought Leadership Structure
1. Hook/controversial statement
2. Current state analysis
3. Trends and data
4. Predictions/vision
5. Implications
6. Call to action

**Length**: 1,500-2,500 words
**Visuals**: Data charts, infographics

## Content Production Workflow

### Planning (Monthly)
1. Review previous month performance
2. Identify top topics and keywords
3. Plan next month's calendar
4. Assign ownership
5. Set deadlines

### Creation
1. **Research**: 1-2 hours per post
2. **Writing**: 2-3 hours per post
3. **Review**: 30 minutes
4. **Design**: 1 hour (visuals)
5. **SEO optimization**: 30 minutes

### Publishing
1. Upload to CMS
2. Add metadata (title, description, keywords)
3. Add images with alt text
4. Internal linking
5. Schedule publication
6. Prepare social promotion

### Promotion
1. Share on all social channels
2. Send in newsletter (if applicable)
3. Share in community
4. Email to relevant customers
5. Repurpose for other formats

### Measurement
1. Track traffic (Google Analytics)
2. Monitor rankings (Ahrefs, SEMrush)
3. Engagement metrics
4. Conversion tracking
5. Monthly report
