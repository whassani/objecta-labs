# ObjectaLabs User Personas

## Overview

Understanding our users helps us build the right features and craft effective messaging. Here are our primary personas.

---

## Persona 1: Sarah - The Overwhelmed Support Manager

### Demographics
- **Age**: 32
- **Role**: Customer Support Manager
- **Company**: Growing SaaS company (50-200 employees)
- **Location**: San Francisco, CA
- **Experience**: 8 years in customer support

### Background
Sarah manages a team of 5 support agents handling 500+ tickets daily. Her company is growing fast, but the support team size isn't keeping pace. Response times are increasing, and customer satisfaction is declining.

### Goals
- Reduce response times
- Handle more tickets with same team size
- Improve customer satisfaction scores
- Reduce team burnout
- Free team to handle complex issues

### Pain Points
- 70% of tickets are repetitive questions
- Team working overtime
- Can't hire fast enough
- Customer complaints increasing
- Management pressure to improve metrics

### Frustrations with Current Solutions
- Basic chatbots are too rigid
- Customers complain about bad bot experiences
- Current solution doesn't understand context
- Can't handle company-specific questions
- Requires IT team to make changes

### Technology Comfort Level
- **Rating**: 6/10
- Can use SaaS tools
- Not a developer
- Needs intuitive interfaces
- Prefers self-service setup

### Decision Criteria
- Easy to implement (< 1 week)
- No coding required
- Handles complex questions
- Integrates with existing tools (Zendesk, Slack)
- Clear ROI

### Ideal Solution
An AI agent that can handle 60-70% of repetitive questions, escalates complex issues to her team, learns from their knowledge base, and works across multiple channels.

### Quote
> "I need something that actually works, not just another chatbot that frustrates customers. And I need to set it up myself - I can't wait weeks for IT."

### How ObjectaLabs Helps
- No-code setup (Sarah can do it herself)
- Learns from existing documentation
- Smart escalation to human agents
- Integrates with her helpdesk
- Deploy in under an hour

### Marketing Messages That Resonate
- "Reduce support volume by 60%"
- "Set up in minutes, not weeks"
- "No IT required"
- "Actually understands your customers"

## Anti-Persona: Who We DON'T Serve (Initially)

### Large Corporations with Custom Requirements
- Need 18-month sales cycles
- Require extensive customization
- Want everything built custom
- **Why not**: Too resource-intensive for early stage

### Users Expecting AGI/Magic
- Think AI can do anything
- Don't want to provide information
- Expect perfection
- **Why not**: Unrealistic expectations, will churn

### Extremely Price-Sensitive Users
- Only want free forever
- Won't upgrade at any price
- Constantly seeking discounts
- **Why not**: Not sustainable customers
